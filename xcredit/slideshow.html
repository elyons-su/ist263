My Dance Journey

Slide 1

I grew up dancing at the same dance studio that my mother attended. It was very special that we had many of the same dance teachers.

Slide 2

I dreamed of becoming a professional ballerina and auditioned for numerous ballet schools and studied in many different places every summer. I attended the Boston Ballet, Gelsey Kirkland Academy of Ballet, and University of North Carolina School of the Arts.

Slide 3

For a very long time I thought I would go to college and study ballet. However, my Junior year of High School after a difficult summer of intensive dancing I changed my mind.

Slide 4

I now am a member of DanceWorks at Syracuse University and dance for fun and to share my passions with friends. I love to perform and the end of the year showcase is always outstanding.

Slide 5

I will always have a passion for dance. Although it is not a career I wish to pursue, it is a passion I will continue to participate in.
